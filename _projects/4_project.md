LE PROSPECT ("THE PROSPECT" in English) is a web-based application specialized in prospect management. I developed this system with my colleague Bensaib Kamel over six months as part of our bachelor's final project. The solution was later used by <a href='https://fecomit.com/'>FecomIT</a> in their prospecting workflow.

In this project, we developed a system to manage the company's prospective clients and follow their progression from prospect to client. Our solution included several features, including:

<ul>
    <li> New prospect creation </li>
    <li> Scoring </li>
    <li> Prospect contact management </li>
    <li> Task management </li>
    <li> Emailing </li>
    <li> Live chat </li>
    <li> Customized features </li>
</ul>
