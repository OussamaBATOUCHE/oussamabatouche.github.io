Me in 2023

I am a Data scientist and PhD condidate at University of Helsinki and part of the Helsinki Urological Cancer Center (HUCC). At HUCC, I am actively developing computational methods to analyse and enhance existing clinical practices related to Prostate Cancer (PCa) treatment at Helsinki University Hospital (HUS). Our primary objective is to prevent unnecessary treatment and overdiagnosis by leveraging clinical and imaging data.

My PhD thesis revolves around developing Machine learning and statistical models to profile PCa and predict its outcomes.
